ABOUT ME

Yaroslav was born with amazing talent, which is visible the moment you lay eyes upon his work.

From an early age Yaroslav was constantly drawing, each time trying to exceed his own abilities and the expectations of people around. He’s constantly experimenting, trying to improve the technique and quality of his work. As it turned out, my whole life was closely connected with creativity. From a very early age, I have always tried to create something new and unique. As a child, I first tried oil painting, and this type of activity has become for me not just a hobby, but something more, a way of self-realization.

High school was the first I knew about architecture and design in general. At this time I took a graphic design course, where I learned to work in many professional software for 2D and 3D design and modeling, for example Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom for color correction. Illustrator, XD, InDesign. For 3D modeling and visualization we have learned Autodesk Maya, 3Ds Max (Including Corona render, Vray, also Substance Painter, Substance Designer and Marvelous Designer for cloth).

Now I want to talk about my work experience. In addition to studying at the university, I worked as a freelance web and UX / UI designer. And now I'm a highly skilled product designer with 3+ years of experience as a freelancer and User Experience and User Interface developer in a Ukrainian digital agency. I have developed a major of interesting and unique projects, such as design of mobile applications, desktop programs, e-commerce web projects and many others. I also had experience in 3d Modeling, logo development and branding.
