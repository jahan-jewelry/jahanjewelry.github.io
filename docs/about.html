ABOUT

Ukrainian artist. Lives and works in Kyiv.

Yaroslav was born with amazing talent, which is visible the moment you lay eyes upon his work. From an early age Yaroslav was constantly drawing, each time trying to exceed his own abilities and the expectations of people around. He’s constantly experimenting, trying to improve the technique and quality of his work.

The preferred methods of work are oil and acrylic painting. Yaroslav primarily works in mixed technique, which includes the elements of abstractionism, realism, impressionism. His pieces make a powerful impression and set the character for any interior.

The artist tries to displays different color combinations, playing with various of compositional solutions, discovering new tricks and style in painting. His work corresponds closely to trends of modern interior design.
