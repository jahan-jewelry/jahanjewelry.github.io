What is Golden Realism?

Golden Realism is not just another regular art movement. This is primarily a breakthrough in painting industry, the high point of evolution in visual components of any picture. Synthesis of complex realistic techniques, correct composition, ideally matched color palette, textual and decorative parts of the background image. All these aspects are perfected in their own way. This is what underlies the Golden Realism, defining it as the most complex, but at the same time most beautiful and ideal art style in existence today.
