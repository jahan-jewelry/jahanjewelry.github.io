About me

Hi, nice to meet you on my website! I’m traditional artist and designer from Ukraine. Since I was a child I am always struggling with creating something new and unique, despite of this my first painting was been created when I was 7 y.o. My dream, and especially goal as an artist is to discover new direction in traditional art and and break the established boundaries of world painting familiar to us.

Looking on my portfolio you can understand that I have many paintings in different styles and genres. I’m always trying experiment with a huge amount of techniques, trying to find something new in painting, expand my abilities as an artist and become a discoverer of a new, still unknown school for us. Purchasing my work at an early stage of artistic career will be a good investment, as in the near future I plan to achieve worldwide fame and increase my influence on the art market in general.
